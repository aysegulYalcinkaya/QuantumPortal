Precision Query

Highly Accurate User Queries, Matchups to RFIs/RFPs

Quantum Ontology makes precision query possible. All of the ingested data from documents, users’ profiles of interests and expertise, and other data entered by members, can be queried easily and precisely. Precision query saves quantum researchers, engineers and stakeholders time and it provides complete, accurate results without all the noise typical of a Google search, for example.

Stakeholders can easily request RFIs with precision from performers. Performers’ data can be easily retrieved with precision from the knowledge graph. Missing information can be acquired from performed by semi-automated generation of requests for information (RFIs). Furthermore, the Quantum Ontology makes it possible to semi-automatically generate requests for proposals (RFPs). Performers responses can be ingested into the knowledge graph for reuse, saving time and effort in the future.

In this first phase this portal is limited to

  • performers’ prototypes and products,
  • user’s (SME) profiles,
but the sky is the limit going forward.
